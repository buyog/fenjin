Fenjin is intended to be a full-featured Fighting Game Engine at some point in the future. Currently, it's just a demo of some of the core underpinning classes, like the Preloader, Sprite animation, etc.

Controls:
arrow keys to move (no physics engine yet)
Weak punch: A – Medium punch: S – Hard punch: D
Weak kick: Z – Medium kick: X – Hard kick: C
